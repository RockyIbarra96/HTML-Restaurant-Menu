Origin Story

Chef and Owner of H-Thai-Ml Bobby Thai opened up his first location in 1966 in Las Vegas. Bobby's mother Peggy Thai inspired Bobby to cook and open up a restaurant. Unfortunately, Peggy past a year before Chef Bobby opened his first location. Chef Bobby honors his mother by using her same recipies in his menu. Chef Bobby prepares the most authentic Thai food on the west coast. You can taste the love in every dish.
